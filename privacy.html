Streamy Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Overview

Streamy is a media player for user-provided legal Xtream Codes, M3U and M3U8 playlists. Streamy does not provide, host, sell, promote or distribute any TV channels, movies, series, paid content or copyrighted media.

Data Collection

Streamy does not sell user data. Streamy does not collect personal information for advertising purposes.

Playlist Credentials

Playlist URLs and Xtream Codes credentials entered by the user are stored locally on the user's device using system storage. Streamy does not share playlist credentials with third parties.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud sync is enabled by the user, favorites, watch progress and library preferences may be synced through the user's private iCloud account. Streamy does not sell this data to advertisers.

Playback Data

Streamy may store local playback progress, favorites and recently watched items on the user's device to provide features such as Continue Watching and Favorites.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring that they have the legal right to access and play the content they add to Streamy.

Demo Playlist

The built-in demo playlist contains only public test streams and legal demo content.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the app developer.
